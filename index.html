Motivation and pride

Forever a Learner

From a young age, I’ve always had a huge variety of interests, from dinosaurs and air balloons to art and baking. I think I’ve always just enjoyed learning new things and finding ways to connect different ideas and concepts together. That’s one trait I’m proud to say I still have today. Despite being a firm believer of “no pain, no gain,” especially when it comes to education and personal growth, I also think there’s something genuinely rewarding about learning. It definitely isn’t always easy to stay curious or motivated, but I take pride in my ability to learn, adapt, and continuously improve. As an engineering student, I enjoy combining technical thinking with creativity and design to create things that are both functional and meaningful.
